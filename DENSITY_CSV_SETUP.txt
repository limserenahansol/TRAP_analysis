TRAP density CSV — required input
================================

RUN_PIPELINE_ALL needs at least one TRAP export CSV (Allen atlas columns + one
column per mouse: "... density (cells/mm^3)").

How to connect your file
------------------------

A) Put the CSV inside this folder (TRAP_pipeline) and list its filename in
   TRAP_cohort_CSVs.txt (one line, no #).

B) Or keep the CSV anywhere on disk: open TRAP_cohort_CSVs.txt and replace the
   line with the FULL path to your file, for example:
   D:\Projects\TRAP\Hansol Lim density channel 561_all.csv

C) If you do not use TRAP_cohort_CSVs.txt: delete or rename it, then set
   C.csvPath in trap_config.m to the full path of your CSV.

TRAP_sample_manifest.csv must list every sample column you want (column_name
must match the CSV header text exactly).
